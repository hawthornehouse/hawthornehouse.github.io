How the House works

Four things we promise every family.

There are good directories in this market, and they'll find you a tutor. We manage the teacher, the relationship, and the outcome — which is a different thing entirely.

We find the right teacher

We pick one teacher for your child and tell you why, in writing. No pile of CVs to sift through yourself.

You get — a written introduction, and the reason behind it.

We handle everything

Scheduling, billing, materials, messages — we deal with all of it, so it never lands on your plate.

You get — one point of contact, one invoice, one calendar.

We keep you in the loop

You'll hear from us before you have to ask — a straight answer on how your child is doing and what's next.

You get — same-day notes after lessons, and a monthly call.

We stand behind the match

If a teacher isn't the right fit, we sort out a replacement quickly — and we cover the cost.

You get — a new teacher within a week, at no extra charge.

The founders

Run by two founders.

Two founders who came through the British education system themselves — with backgrounds in medicine and law — and who care deeply about teaching done properly.

Nayyab

Co-founder

Nayyab studied medicine in the UK and practises as a family medicine doctor in Dubai. She brings the same instincts medicine demands to Hawthorne House: listening closely, knowing when something needs to change, and leading every family relationship from the first conversation onward. She makes a point of understanding each child before anything else, and gives parents one person who knows their child's programme and is always easy to reach.

Bilal

Co-founder

Bilal has a legal background, having studied law in the UK, and has built a career at the intersection of law and emerging technology. Currently serving as AI and Legal Technology Lead at Thomson Reuters MENA, Bilal brings the same rigour and attention to detail to Hawthorne House — overseeing the teacher roster, maintaining the standards every family is promised, and making sure every family feels genuinely looked after.
